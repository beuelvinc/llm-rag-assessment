**Baked Salmon with Sweet Potatoes and Broccoli**

**Ingredients:**
- Salmon fillets
- Sweet potatoes, cubed
- Broccoli florets
- Olive oil
- Lemon juice
- Garlic powder, salt, and pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat your oven to 400F (200C).
2. Toss sweet potatoes in olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Spread them on a baking sheet.
3. Bake for about 15 minutes, then add broccoli and salmon to the sheet.
4. Drizzle lemon juice over the salmon and veggies, season as desired, and bake for another 15-20 minutes until everything is cooked through.
5. Enjoy!
